About Me

Since when I was a child I had interest in technology, specially because of games. In 2008 I started studying Bachelor in Computer Science in UNIFESP (Universidade Federal de Sao Paulo - Brazil) and I graduated in 2011.

While I was in the college, I had the opportunity to work as intern in Internal Revenue Service of Brazil for 1 year (2010) and I also worked as intern in the Municipal Hospital of Sao Jose dos Campos, SP in Brazil, for 1 year (2011).

When I worked in the hospital, I learned a lot about the health area. I had to work with doctors, nurses, students and pacients. Because of that I decided to make my final project (TCC) be about magnetic resonance imaging, to be more especific, I developed a software that received all the images and created a 3D model, which could be handled. Basically, after rendering the model, it could be rotated and zoomed, and the doctors could just analyze this model, intead of having to handle with lots of images.

After graduating I started working in Touch Tecnology, where I stayed for 5 years (2012-2017) as Java developer.

At the moment, I am studying Web and Mobile Develop at Cornerstone in Vancouver, BC, Canada.
